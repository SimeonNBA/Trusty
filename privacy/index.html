Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-05-06

Trusty AI is a Chrome extension that flags risky contract addresses on the websites you already visit. This page explains, in plain English, exactly what the extension collects and what it doesn't.

What the extension does

When you visit a supported page, Trusty scans the page text in your browser to find cryptocurrency contract addresses (EVM 0x… and Solana base58 mints). For each address it finds, Trusty fetches a safety verdict from our backend. Hover the pill for a summary; click for the full report.

Supported sites today:

  • x.com and twitter.com
  • reddit.com, old.reddit.com, new.reddit.com, sh.reddit.com
  • dexscreener.com

What we collect

On-device only — never leaves your browser

  • Page text, used purely to find contract addresses. Nothing stored, nothing transmitted.
  • An in-memory cache of recent scan results, scoped to your current page session.
  • Your tier preferences (paid wallet address, expiry timestamp), saved in chrome.storage.local.

Sent to our backend (api.trustyai.tech)

  • The contract address you scanned (e.g. 0xabc…123).
  • A best-guess chain identifier (bsc, ethereum, solana).
  • Standard HTTP headers your browser automatically sends to any website.

Sent to public BNB Smart Chain RPC nodes (only when you verify a paid wallet)

  • Your BSC wallet address, embedded in a standard ERC-20 balanceOf read call, sent to a public RPC endpoint (Binance, defibit, publicnode, ninicoin).
🛡️ That's it.

We do not send the page URL, tweet content, your X/Reddit account, your IP-derived identity, or any other identifying data. The contract address you scan is public on-chain information.

What we do not collect

  • Your X/Twitter account, posts, follows, DMs, or profile data.
  • Your Reddit account, posts, comments, or karma.
  • Any text other than the contract address you scanned.
  • Which sites you visit or how long you spend on them.
  • Cookies, fingerprinting, or third-party analytics from inside the extension.

We never sell, share, or rent any data.

Server-side caching

Our backend caches scan results for ~5 minutes per contract address to reduce load on the data sources we query (DexScreener, GoPlus, and others). The cache is keyed only by the contract address — it contains no information about who requested the scan.

Paid tier and wallet addresses

If you unlock the paid tier by linking a $TRUSTY wallet address, that address is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. We make periodic read-only calls (every 30 days, or when you click "Re-verify") to public BSC RPC endpoints to check whether the address still holds the required $TRUSTY balance.

  • We never request signatures, transactions, approvals, seed phrases, or private keys.
  • We never broadcast anything on your behalf.
  • The wallet address is not sent to our servers — only to public BSC RPC nodes, which already see this kind of public on-chain query.
  • The address is removed from local storage when you click "Re-verify" and replace it, or when you uninstall the extension.

Permissions, explained

  • storage — to remember your tier and recent scans on your device.
  • https://trustyai.tech/* — for the website and click-through reports.
  • https://api.trustyai.tech/* — to call the Trusty scanning API.
  • https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/*, https://bsc-dataseed1.defibit.io/*, https://bsc.publicnode.com/*, https://bsc-dataseed1.ninicoin.io/* — to verify your $TRUSTY balance via public RPC nodes.
  • Content scripts on x.com, twitter.com, reddit.com, dexscreener.com — to detect contract addresses on the pages you visit.

The extension does not have access to the rest of the web. It cannot read your other tabs, your bookmarks, your downloads, or any site that isn't listed above.

Children

Trusty is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.
