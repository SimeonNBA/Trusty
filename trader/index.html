How it works

Every 10 minutes the agent pulls the top BSC memecoins by 24h volume, keeps only those Trusty rates APE and that show social traction (X chatter or Binance Square mentions), and paper-buys $100 each. Positions exit on +50% take-profit, −30% stop-loss, a 48h timeout, or if Trusty's verdict flips to RUN. A 2% slippage + gas cost is charged on every entry and exit, so the P&L is honest.
