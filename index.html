📅 The Timeline
2017
🏗️ Built in Three Weeks LAUNCH
Ukrainian developer Viktor Radchenko had a problem: he'd aped into a bunch of ICOs and had nowhere to safely store his ERC-20 tokens on mobile. So he did what any based builder would do — he built it himself. Starting in September 2017, he coded the first version in roughly three weeks. Trust Wallet launched in November 2017 as an iOS-only app focused on Ethereum and ERC-20 tokens.
🛡️
The whole reason Trust Wallet exists is because Viktor wanted to safely hold his ICO bags. Relatable energy honestly. Built different from day one.
2018
🤝 Binance Buys the Shield ACQUISITION
Within months of launch, monthly users crossed 20,000, and Viktor knew he needed help. Co-founder Maxim Rasputin joined to build the Android version. After ETH support blew up the user count past 100,000, Binance came knocking. On July 31, 2018, Binance acquired Trust Wallet in its first-ever acquisition. CZ himself called it "simply the best" mobile wallet. Crucially, Viktor kept autonomy to keep building the product his way.
🛡️
Getting acquired by the world's biggest exchange before your first birthday? That's a speedrun nobody else has done. WAGMI energy from the start.
2019
🔐 First Security Incident SECURITY
No project's history is complete without a rekt chapter. Hackers compromised several Trust Wallet servers used for app updates and data sync, injecting malware into a fake update. The team caught it fast, alerted the community, and rebuilt update verification from scratch. A painful but important moment that shaped Trust Wallet's serious approach to security going forward.
🛡️
Every OG project gets tested. What matters is how you respond. Trust Wallet patched up and came back stronger, ser.
2020
🪙 TWT Token Goes Live + Staking TOKEN
The Trust Wallet Token (TWT) launched as a BEP-2 asset on Binance Chain, then relaunched as a BEP-20 token on Binance Smart Chain in October 2020 — with 99% of the original supply burned. Total supply capped at 1 billion TWT. Interestingly, Viktor said TWT was originally created as a "fun thing" for a referral system, not as a serious token. The staking feature also launched this year, pushing adoption to new highs. Trust Wallet ranked #6 in global crypto app downloads.
🛡️
99% supply burn on launch. Imagine being that based with tokenomics. Most projects today could never ser.
2021
🖼️ DeFi + NFTs Enter the Chat FEATURE
The DeFi summer aftermath supercharged Trust Wallet's feature set. The team added full DeFi ecosystem access directly from mobile — swaps, yield, protocols — all without leaving the app. Then came NFT marketplace integration, letting users mint, buy, and sell NFTs. The wallet went from a token storage app to a full Web3 portal in your pocket. This was the era that made Trust Wallet essential for the average degen.
🛡️
2021 was when the average degen realized they needed a proper wallet, not just an exchange account. Trust Wallet was ready. Based timing ngl.
2022
🔄 New CEO + Browser Extension GROWTH
Viktor Radchenko stepped down as CEO in March 2022 to focus on family, handing the reins to Eowyn Chen, former Binance Marketing VP. The browser extension launched, taking Trust Wallet from mobile-only to cross-platform. But November 2022 brought another security incident — a WebAssembly vulnerability in the browser extension resulted in around $170,000 in losses. Trust Wallet reimbursed affected users and patched the vulnerability.
🛡️
New CEO, new chapter. Eowyn Chen came in swinging. Reimbursing users for the security exploit? That's what separates based projects from rug protocols.
2023
💵 Fiat Off-Ramp + Security Scanner FEATURE
April 2023 saw Trust Wallet partner with MoonPay and Ramp, letting users convert crypto to fiat directly within the wallet — no more sending to a CEX just to cash out. The in-app Security Scanner also went live, checking transaction destination addresses against a blacklist in real time. By year-end the scanner had blocked hundreds of millions in potentially harmful transactions.
🛡️
A wallet that checks if you're about to get rekt BEFORE you send? That's the kind of fren energy this space needs more of.
2024 — 2025
🚀 200M+ Installs & The Next Era GROWTH
Trust Wallet hit 140 million downloads by September 2024, then blew past 200 million by March 2025 — capturing over 35% market share of global crypto wallet downloads. Revenue grew 4x year-over-year from 2023 to 2024, marking the first 8-digit profit year. In September 2025, a bold new roadmap dropped: the goal is 1 billion users by 2030. Trust Premium loyalty program, TWT LitePaper, Trust Moon accelerator, and perpetual trading up to 100x leverage all launched in this era. Today, 1 in 3 crypto holders uses Trust Wallet.

From zero to 200 million installs in 8 years. That's not a startup anymore fren — that's infrastructure. The whole ecosystem runs on Trust Wallet's back. WAGMI.

📅 The Timeline
Oct 2008
📄 Satoshi Drops the Whitepaper ORIGIN
During the 2008 global financial crisis, a mysterious entity called Satoshi Nakamoto publishes "Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System" on a cryptography mailing list. Nine pages that would change money forever. No real name, no face, no institution. Just math. The timing — a banking collapse — was not an accident.
🛡️
Imagine dropping a world-changing idea with no LinkedIn profile and no VC backing. Based behavior. The OG anon dev.
Jan 2009
⛏️ Genesis Block Mined BLOCK 0
Satoshi mines Bitcoin block #0. Embedded in it: "The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks." A timestamp. A flex. A forever receipt. The first 50 BTC are created — and deliberately unspendable. Total market cap: $0.00. Nobody cares. The network has exactly one user.
🛡️
The newspaper headline in block zero is the most legendary easter egg in tech history. Satoshi said "banks bad" in immutable code. Respect.
May 2010
🍕 The Pizza That Haunts Us All MOST EXPENSIVE MEAL
Laszlo Hanyecz posts on BitcoinTalk offering 10,000 BTC for two pizzas. Someone takes him up on it. Papa John's is delivered. History is made. Those 10,000 BTC are worth hundreds of millions of dollars today. Laszlo has said publicly he has no regrets — he was just happy the transaction worked. We celebrate Bitcoin Pizza Day every May 22nd in his honor. Or horror.
🛡️
The most expensive pizza ever ordered. Also technically the first proof that Bitcoin was actual money. Laszlo is both the goat and the cautionary tale.
2011–2013
🏔️ First Bull Run & Mt. Gox Dominance WILD WEST
BTC goes from $1 to $1,000+. Mt. Gox handles 70% of all Bitcoin trades at peak. Litecoin launches (2011) as "digital silver." Ripple (2012) arrives. The Silk Road makes crypto infamous with the mainstream press. This era produces crypto's first millionaires and its first con men. Often the same person.
🛡️
70% of all BTC trades going through one exchange called "Magic: The Gathering Online Exchange" rebranded as a crypto exchange. What could go wrong?
Feb 2014
💥 Mt. Gox Collapses — 850,000 BTC Gone REKT
The world's largest Bitcoin exchange is hacked. 850,000 BTC — worth ~$450M at the time, billions today — disappears. CEO Mark Karpelès becomes public enemy #1. BTC crashes 80%. Most people give up. The post-mortem takes years. Some of the coins were eventually recovered. Karpelès is found guilty of data manipulation. Crypto's first great lesson: not your keys, not your coins.
🛡️
This is literally why self-custody wallets exist. Mt. Gox is the reason Viktor built Trust Wallet. NGMI if you leave your coins on exchanges ser.
Jul 2015
⟠ Ethereum Launches — Programmable Money GAME CHANGER
Vitalik Buterin, 21 years old, launches Ethereum mainnet. Smart contracts mean money can have rules, conditions, and logic. It's not just a currency — it's a programmable blockchain. ICOs, DeFi, NFTs, DAOs — none of it exists without this moment. ETH trades at $0.43 on launch. The world has no idea what just happened.
🛡️
A 21-year-old invents programmable money. Every DeFi protocol, every NFT collection, every meme coin ecosystem traces back to this one launch. Vitalik is built different.
2017
🚀 ICO Mania — Everyone Launches a Token BULL RUN
Initial Coin Offerings explode. $5.6 billion raised via ICOs in 2017 alone. Whitepaper + Telegram group + website = instant millions. ETH hits $1,400. BTC hits $20,000 for the first time. Crypto Twitter is born. "Have fun staying poor" enters the chat. 99% of ICO projects are scams. Nobody cares at the time.
🛡️
Peak ICO era had people raising $50M for a PDF with stick figures in it. The degen spirit was there but the DYOR was not. Lessons were learned the expensive way.
2018
📉 The Great Crypto Winter REKT SEASON
BTC crashes from $20k to $3k. ETH drops 95% from peak. Every ICO project dies or goes dark. Crypto Twitter goes quiet. Mainstream media declares crypto dead for the 4th time. The diamond hands who stayed through the winter would be rewarded in 2020-21. 80%+ of retail sold the bottom and never came back. They are the exit liquidity lore of this era.
🛡️
Every degen who survived 2018 bears came out the other side with unbreakable conviction. The bear market is where legends are forged fren.
Summer 2020
🦄 DeFi Summer — The Yield Farming Explosion DEFI BORN
Compound launches governance token COMP. Suddenly you get paid to borrow money. Yield farming is born. Total Value Locked in DeFi goes from $1B to $15B in months. Uniswap V2 launches AMM liquidity pools. Every degen becomes a liquidity provider. Gas fees hit $200 per transaction. Nobody cares — the yields are insane. This is the summer that changes everything about crypto's future.
🛡️
DeFi Summer 2020 was the first time crypto actually delivered on its "banks but better" promise. Aping into liquidity pools at 3am became a personality. Respect.
2020–2021
🐶 Meme Coins Go Mainstream — DOGE & SHIB MEME ERA
Dogecoin, originally a joke from 2013, becomes a $80B asset at peak. Elon Musk tweets about it relentlessly. Shiba Inu launches as a "DOGE killer" and hits a $40B market cap. The world learns that narrative and community matter infinitely more than fundamentals. This changes how everyone thinks about value in crypto forever. Meme coin season is born.
🛡️
A dog meme coin reaching $80B market cap while Elon tweets about it from a Saturday Night Live stage is the most crypto thing that has ever happened. We don't deserve this timeline.
2021
🖼️ NFT Mania — Bored Apes, Punks & the $69M JPEG NFT SUMMER
Beeple sells an NFT for $69 million at Christie's. Bored Ape Yacht Club launches at 0.08 ETH (~$200), sells out in hours. Floor price eventually hits 100 ETH. CryptoPunks trade for millions. Justin Bieber, Steph Curry, and Eminem buy ape JPEGs. Your profile picture becomes your identity and your social class signal. $40B+ in NFT volume in 2021 alone. Most of it is now near-worthless. The art lives on though.

We told people about a JPEGs-as-identity economy and they said we were crazy. Then celebrities paid 6 figures for cartoon apes. Nobody was ready ser.
May 2022
💀 LUNA/UST Collapses — $60B Wiped in 72 Hours CATASTROPHIC REKT
Terra Luna's algorithmic stablecoin UST loses its dollar peg. The death spiral begins. Do Kwon's "stablecoin" implodes. $60 billion in value evaporates in 72 hours. LUNA goes from $100 to $0.00001. Life savings wiped. Hedge funds blow up. Do Kwon tweets "I'm not running." He was running. Arrested in Serbia months later. The most catastrophic algo-stablecoin collapse in history.
🛡️
$60B gone in 72 hours. Do Kwon had a "Luna classic" plan while people lost everything. This is why Trusty checks supply control first. Algorithmic = danger ser.

Sam Bankman-Fried's FTX — the second-largest crypto exchange — collapses in days after CZ tweets about selling FTT. $8 billion in customer funds vanish. SBF is arrested in the Bahamas. Every VC, celebrity, and institution that backed FTX goes silent. The "effective altruism" era of crypto ends catastrophically. Self-custody goes from optional to mandatory. "Not your keys, not your coins" becomes the most quoted phrase in crypto history.

FTX is the final, definitive proof that centralized exchanges are not your friend. Trusty exists precisely so you never have to trust someone else with your coins fren.

$DOGE $SHIB $WIF $BONK $FLOKI $NEIRO

$PEPE $BRETT $WIF $PENGU $ANDY

The PENGUIN narrative in January 2026 showed a new evolution: a political moment (White House post) triggering an existing coin rather than a new launch. The coin already had community from the documentary meme — the White House post was pure gasoline.
$TRUMP $MELANIA $BODEN $MAGA

Only before the political event — not after. Watch election calendars, political debate schedules, major policy announcements. The entry is hours before mainstream coverage, not after. Very small position size only.

After the political event already happened. If the politician has already acknowledged the coin — that's usually the top. Any coin claiming official political endorsement is lying and likely a rug.

Political news cycles. Track Google Trends for political terms — when a political figure's name spikes in search volume, related coins follow within hours. Monitor Polymarket for political events.
